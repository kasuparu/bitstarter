Want to know real estate property prices and even find some opportunities for rent or sale?

Welcome to my garage project, OpenEstateMap. Here you can find a great "heatmap" of real estate prices and even see the animation of its latest changes.

See highlighted property that is significantly cheaper than its surroundings. But what if it's just underpriced by the owner?

The main goal of my project is to create an easy and free to use service. I use famous OpenStreetMap for geography and some "spicy" MongoDB aggregations to ease the search and provide analytics. All information is gathered from Real Estate Agencies and is publicly available.

I want OpenEstateMap to cover the territory of European Union, but for now it's only Lithuania - a small country with overheated estate market. Just because I need something to start with.

I am neither affilated with any real estate agency nor have enough money to fully dedicate myself to this project so any help will be appreciated.
